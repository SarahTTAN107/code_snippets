Vietnamese people in Finland form one of the country's largest groups of Southeast Asian people. 
According to Statistics Finland, in 2017 there are 10,817 people with a Vietnamese background, 
9,872 people whose mother tongue is Vietnamese[5], 
,012 people who have been born in Vietnam, 
and 5,603 people with Vietnamese citizenship[2] residing in Finland. 
The Vietnamese-Finnish community includes both ethnic Vietnamese and Sino-Vietnamese.[6]
